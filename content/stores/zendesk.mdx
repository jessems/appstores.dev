## Overview

Zendesk Marketplace is the official extension hub for Zendesk's customer service platform. With over 1,700 apps and integrations, it has grown from 500 apps to its current size in just a few years. Over 58% of Zendesk customers use at least one marketplace app.

## Key Statistics

- **1,700+ apps** and integrations available
- **1,176 free apps** - most integrations are free to install
- **58% of customers** use at least one marketplace app
- **85% of Zendesk's ARR** comes from customers using partner integrations
- **50% of customers** use more than one app

## App Categories

- Productivity & collaboration
- Analytics & reporting
- Marketing & automation
- CRMs & sales
- IT & operations
- AI & bots

## Development Requirements

1. Request sponsored test account
2. Build and test app
3. Set up global OAuth client
4. Create brand assets and content
5. Set up payment options (if applicable)
6. Register organization
7. Create Marketplace profile
8. Submit app for review
