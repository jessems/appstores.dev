## Overview

VMware Tanzu Platform Marketplace (formerly Pivotal Services Marketplace) is the official services marketplace for Tanzu Platform for Cloud Foundry. With 50+ data services and platform capabilities, it provides cloud-native application development infrastructure.

## Key Statistics

- **50+ services** (estimated)
- Enterprise cloud platform
- Acquired by Broadcom (Nov 2023)
- Tanzu Platform 10 (Explore 2024)

## Recent Changes (2024-2025)

- VMware acquired by Broadcom (Nov 2023)
- Tanzu Labs shutdown (Jan 2025)
- Pivotal Tracker retired (Apr 2025)
- Tanzu Platform 10 launched
