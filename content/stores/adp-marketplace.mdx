## Overview

ADP Marketplace launched in October 2014 as the first human capital management (HCM) marketplace. With 700+ prebuilt integrations, it connects ADP's payroll and HR platform with third-party business solutions.

## Key Statistics

- **700+ prebuilt integrations**
- **AI integrations** for HR chatbots
- First HCM marketplace (2014)
- Grown from 135 solutions (2018) to 700+ (2025)

## Historical Growth

- 2014: Launched with initial integrations
- 2018: 135 integrated solutions, 250+ total
- 2025: 700+ prebuilt integrations

## Integration Categories

- Payroll
- Time & labor management
- Benefits administration
- Recruiting & onboarding
- Learning management
- Performance management
- Workforce analytics

## Key Features

- Automatic data sharing
- ADP API Central for custom integrations
- Pre-vetted solutions
- One-click connections
- Enterprise security

## Development

Build integrations using:
- ADP APIs (HR, payroll, time)
- ADP Marketplace Integration Guides
- OAuth 2.0 authentication
- Webhooks
- ADP Developer Resources
