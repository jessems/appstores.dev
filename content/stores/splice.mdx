## Overview

Splice is the leading platform for music production tools, offering rent-to-own plugins, 2M+ royalty-free samples, and the INSTRUMENT virtual instrument. It enables producers to access industry-standard tools through affordable monthly payments.

## Key Statistics

- **100+ plugins** via rent-to-own
- **2M+ samples** in library
- **1,200+ presets** in INSTRUMENT plugin
- Acquired Spitfire Audio

## INSTRUMENT Plugin (2025)

Launched October 2025:
- Cloud-connected virtual instrument
- 1,200+ multi-sampled presets
- 489 free presets in 66 packs
- Based on Spitfire Audio library

### Pricing

- **Free Plan**: Hundreds of presets, monthly updates
- **Subscription**: $12.99/month for full 1,200+ catalog
