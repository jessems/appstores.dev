## Overview

The Apple App Store, launched on July 10, 2008, revolutionized mobile app distribution. It remains the exclusive way to distribute apps to iOS, iPadOS, macOS (for Mac App Store apps), watchOS, and tvOS devices for most users.

## Key Features for Developers

### App Store Connect
Apple's developer portal provides:
- App analytics and sales reports
- TestFlight for beta testing
- App Store optimization tools
- Financial reports and tax documentation
- Team management and role assignments

### TestFlight
Apple's official beta testing platform allows:
- Up to 10,000 external testers
- Up to 100 internal testers
- 90-day testing period per build
- Automatic build distribution

### App Clips
Light-weight app experiences (under 15MB) that can be accessed without full app installation.
