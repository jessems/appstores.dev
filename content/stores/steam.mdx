## Overview

Steam, launched in 2003, dominates the PC gaming market. It offers not just game distribution but a complete ecosystem including community features, streaming, and hardware integration through Steam Deck.

## Steamworks Features

### Community Features
- Steam Workshop for mods
- Community Hub and forums
- Trading Cards and badges
- Steam Broadcasting

### Technical Features
- Cloud saves
- Achievement system
- Multiplayer infrastructure
- Anti-cheat systems
- Remote Play Together
