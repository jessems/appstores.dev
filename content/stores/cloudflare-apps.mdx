## Overview

Cloudflare Apps was the marketplace for third-party applications on Cloudflare's edge network. As of December 2024, the traditional Apps marketplace is transitioning to a new Workers-based experience.

## Marketplace Transition

After December 20, 2024:
- Traditional Apps deprecated
- Apps with Workers recommended
- New Apps experience in development
- Contact: apps-marketplace-support@cloudflare.com

## Cloudflare Workers

Workers provides serverless compute at the edge:
- **500 Workers** per Paid plan account
- **100 Workers** per Free plan account
- Workers for Platforms for larger needs

## Apps with Workers

Bundle complex worker logic for marketplace:
- Worker-powered experiences
- Few-click deployment
- Cloudflare Apps distribution

## Development Stack

- JavaScript/TypeScript
- Rust (via WebAssembly)
- Python (via Pyodide)
- Wrangler CLI
- Workers AI
