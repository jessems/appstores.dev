## Overview

Matomo Marketplace is the official plugin marketplace for Matomo's privacy-focused analytics platform. With 40+ core plugins and 100+ community plugins, it provides an open marketplace similar to Google Play or App Store for analytics extensions.

- **100+ plugins** (estimated)
- **40+ core plugins** maintained by Matomo
- **18 premium plugins** in Premium Bundle
- Open, community-driven marketplace
