## Overview

Envato Market is the world's largest marketplace for digital creative assets, founded in 2006 in Melbourne, Australia. Acquired by Shutterstock in 2024, it continues to operate as a standalone platform hosting over 19 million items.

## Key Statistics

- **19M+ items** in total library
- **50k+ WordPress themes**
- **8k+ WordPress plugins**
- Prices starting from $6 (plugins) to $13 (themes)
- Lifetime updates included

## Envato Marketplaces

- **ThemeForest**: WordPress and web themes
- **CodeCanyon**: Plugins and code scripts
- **VideoHive**: Stock video and motion graphics
- **AudioJungle**: Music and audio
- **GraphicRiver**: Graphics and design assets
- **PhotoDune**: Stock photography
- **3DOcean**: 3D models and assets

## Revenue Share

### Exclusive Authors
- Start at 62.5% revenue share
- Scale to 87.5% based on lifetime earnings
- Best rates for top performers

### Non-Exclusive Authors
- 45% of each sale
- Can sell on multiple platforms

## Envato Elements

Separate subscription service offering:
- Unlimited downloads for subscribers
- Flat-rate for authors based on downloads
