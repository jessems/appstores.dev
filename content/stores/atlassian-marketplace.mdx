## Overview

Atlassian Marketplace launched in 2012 and has grown to over 6,000 apps serving millions of users of Jira, Confluence, Bitbucket, and Trello.

## Platform Options

### Forge (Recommended)
- Serverless app platform
- 0% revenue share until $1M lifetime
- Required for best commission rates

### Connect
- Traditional cloud app framework
- Currently 15%, increasing to 25% by October 2026
- More flexibility but higher fees

## Revenue Share Changes (2026)

| Date | Forge Apps | Connect Apps |
|------|------------|--------------|
| Current | 15% | 15% |
| April 2026 | 16% | 20% |
| October 2026 | 17% | 25% |

**Note:** Forge apps get 0% until reaching $1M lifetime revenue across all Forge apps.
