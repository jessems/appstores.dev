## Overview

Gemini Gems are custom AI experts that users can create within Google's Gemini AI platform. Unveiled at Google I/O in August 2024, Gems allow users to build personalized assistants tailored to specific tasks, from brainstorming partners to coding helpers.

## Key Features

### Workspace Integration
Gems are deeply integrated with Google Workspace:
- Available in Docs, Sheets, Slides side panels
- Accessible from Gmail and Google Drive
- Seamless context sharing across apps

### Gem Creation
Users can create Gems by:
- Selecting from premade templates (Learning Coach, Brainstormer, Career Guide, Writing Editor, Coding Partner)
- Creating custom Gems with natural language instructions
- Defining personality, expertise, and response style

### Sharing Capabilities
As of September 2025, Gems support sharing via:
- Drive-style permissions (Viewer or Editor)
- Direct link sharing
- Organization-wide distribution for Workspace accounts

## Super Gems (Coming Soon)

Google is developing "Super Gems" which will:
- Function as pre-built AI workflows
- Allow visual creation of AI apps without coding
- Enable publishing to a public Super Gems Marketplace
- Support monetization for creators

## Premade Gems

Google provides several premade Gems:
- **Learning Coach**: Personalized tutoring and study assistance
- **Brainstormer**: Creative ideation and problem-solving
- **Career Guide**: Professional development and job search help
- **Writing Editor**: Content review and improvement
- **Coding Partner**: Programming assistance and debugging

## Requirements

- Gemini Advanced, Business, or Enterprise subscription
- Google account
- Available in 180+ countries
