## Overview

Aptoide is one of the oldest and largest alternative Android app stores. Its unique distributed model allows developers and companies to create their own app stores within the Aptoide ecosystem.

## Unique Features

### Custom Stores
Developers can create branded stores with their own apps, allowing direct distribution to users.

### AppCoins Protocol
Aptoide developed AppCoins, a blockchain-based payment protocol for app transactions.
