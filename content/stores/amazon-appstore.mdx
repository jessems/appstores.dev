## Overview

Amazon Appstore is Amazon's proprietary app store for Android devices and Amazon's Fire product line. It offers developers access to millions of customers across Amazon's device ecosystem.

## Unique Features

### Amazon Coins
A virtual currency that customers can use to purchase apps and in-app items at a discount, while developers still receive full revenue.

### Merch by Amazon Integration
Developers can integrate merchandise sales directly within their apps.

### Fire Device Optimization
Apps can be optimized for Fire tablets, Fire TV, and Echo Show devices for enhanced user experiences.
