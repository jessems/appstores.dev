## Overview

BigCommerce Apps Marketplace offers 1,300+ apps and integrations for BigCommerce's enterprise e-commerce platform. Apps are vetted and organized into a tiered partner system (Standard, Preferred, Elite).

## Key Statistics

- **1,300+ apps** and integrations
- **250+ marketing apps**
- **9.72% AI-powered** apps (growing)
- **65.42% US-based** partners

## Partner Tiers

### Standard Partners
Basic app listings with standard benefits

### Preferred Partners
Enhanced visibility and support

### Elite Partners
Premium tier for differentiated vertical solutions or core platform functionality

## App Categories

- Advertising & marketing
- Analytics & reporting
- Email marketing
- Mobile optimization
- Multichannel listings
- Payments & security
- Shipping & fulfillment
- Customer service

## Development

Build apps using:
- BigCommerce REST API
- GraphQL Storefront API
- OAuth 2.0 authentication
- Webhooks
- Single-click app installation
