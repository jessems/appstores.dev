## Overview

Zoho Marketplace is the official extension platform for Zoho's comprehensive business software suite. With 2,500+ extensions as of early 2025, it has grown significantly from the 1,000+ milestone celebrated in 2024.

## Key Statistics

- **2,500+ extensions** available (2025)
- **30,000+ monthly installations**
- **100,000+ business users** reached globally
- **374,000+ total installations**
- **29 Zoho products** supported
- **40+ business categories**

- Zoho CRM

- Zoho Analytics
