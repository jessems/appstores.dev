## Overview

Stripe App Marketplace launched in 2022 and has grown to 341+ apps from 302 ISV partners. It extends Stripe's payment infrastructure with apps that integrate directly into the Stripe Dashboard.

- **302 ISV partners**
- **5.57% growth** in last 30 days
- **7.04% AI-powered** apps
- **75% US-based** partners
- More than doubled since launch (70 to 341)
