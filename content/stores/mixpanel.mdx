## Overview

Mixpanel Integrations connects Mixpanel's product analytics platform with 50+ native integrations and direct data warehouse connectivity. It enables unified event tracking and user analytics across web, mobile, and backend systems.

## Key Statistics

- **50+ native integrations**
- Data warehouse connectivity
- 8,000+ apps via Zapier
- Direct warehouse sync support

## Integration Methods

### APIs
Send user and event data to and from Mixpanel.

### SDKs
Event logging directly from your app.

### Webhooks
Trigger workflows based on Mixpanel events.

### Third-Party Connectors
Zapier, Make, Integrately, Zoho Flow.
