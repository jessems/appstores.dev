## Overview

Amazon Alexa Skills Store launched in 2015 alongside the Amazon Echo and has grown to over 100,000 skills globally. It powers voice interactions on Echo devices, Fire TV, third-party speakers, and 100,000+ compatible smart home devices.

## Key Statistics

- **100,000+ skills** available globally
- **100,000+ smart home devices** compatible
- **12+ countries** with localized stores
- Alexa Developer Rewards for top skills

## Skill Categories

- Smart home control
- Games & trivia
- Music & audio
- News & information
- Productivity
- Shopping
- Health & fitness
- Education

## Monetization Options

### In-Skill Purchases
- One-time purchases
- Consumables
- Subscriptions
- 70% revenue share to developers

### Alexa Developer Rewards
Amazon pays bonuses for highly-engaged skills based on usage metrics.

## Development

Build skills using:
- Alexa Skills Kit (ASK)
- Node.js, Python, or Java SDKs
- Alexa-hosted (free backend)
- Voice design best practices
