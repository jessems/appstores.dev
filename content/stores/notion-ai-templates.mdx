## Overview

The Notion Template Marketplace hosts 30,000+ templates, with a growing category of AI-powered templates that leverage Notion AI. Creators can build, share, and sell templates that incorporate AI capabilities for content creation, analysis, and productivity enhancement.

## AI Template Categories

### AI Writing Tools
- Automated drafting assistance
- Tone adjustment capabilities
- Translation integrations
- Content summarization

### AI Prompts
- Curated prompt libraries
- Workflow-specific prompt collections
- Creative and professional prompts
- Prompt engineering guides

### AI-Powered Tools
- Data analysis templates
- Research organization
- Meeting note automation
- Project planning with AI

## Notion AI Features

Notion AI is integrated throughout the workspace:
- **Write**: Draft, brainstorm, and expand content
- **Edit**: Improve, translate, and adjust tone
- **Summarize**: Condense documents and notes
- **Extract**: Pull key information from content
- **Explain**: Clarify complex topics

- Project trackers with AI insights

## Selling Templates

### Requirements
1. Create Notion creator profile at notion.so/profile
2. Join waitlist for marketplace selling
3. Get approved by Notion team
4. Onboard with Stripe for payments

### Pricing
- Creators set their own prices
- Notion takes 20% commission
- Stripe handles payment processing
- Direct deposits to creator accounts

### Best Practices
- Provide comprehensive documentation
- Include setup instructions
- Offer customization guidance
- Create video walkthroughs
- Respond to buyer questions
