## Overview

ServiceNow Store launched in 2015 and has grown to 500+ NOW Certified applications. It extends ServiceNow's enterprise workflow platform with pre-built integrations for ITSM, HR, security, and operations.

## Key Statistics

- **500+ apps** available (estimated)
- **59% AI/ML apps** added in 2024
- **45+ free apps** available
- **48% of listings** from top developers
- Grown from 80 apps (2015) to 500+ (2025)

## Market Growth

The ServiceNow Store Apps market is projected to grow from $14.74B (2025) to $196.11B (2034), reflecting 33.30% CAGR and massive enterprise adoption.
