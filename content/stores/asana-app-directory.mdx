## Overview

Asana Apps offers 200+ integrations for Asana's project management platform. With 70% of Asana users integrating with at least three other tools, the directory is a key part of the Asana experience.

## Key Statistics

- **200+ integrations** available
- **70% of users** integrate with 3+ tools
- **30% faster** project completion with integrations
- **60% of users** say integrations reduce meetings

## Integration Categories

- Communications (Slack, Microsoft Teams)
- Files (Google Drive, Dropbox, Box)
- Productivity (Time tracking, automation)
- Connectors (Zapier, Make)

- OAuth 2.0 authentication
