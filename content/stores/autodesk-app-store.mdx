## Overview

Autodesk App Store (formerly Autodesk Exchange Apps) is the official marketplace for plugins and add-ons extending Autodesk design software. With 4,000+ apps, it serves architects, engineers, designers, and 3D artists worldwide.

## Key Statistics

- **4,000+ apps** available (as of 2024)
  - 1,321 free apps
  - 906 trial apps
  - 1,623 paid apps
- **1,000+ AutoCAD apps** specifically

## Supported Products

- AutoCAD (and verticals: Civil 3D, MEP, Architecture, Electrical)
- Revit
- 3ds Max
- Maya
- Inventor
- Fusion 360
- BIM 360
- Navisworks
- Vault
- Robot Structural Analysis
- Alias

## App Types

- Plugins and add-ons
- Stand-alone applications
- Training tutorials
- E-books and documentation
- Productivity tools

## Development

Build apps using:
- Autodesk Platform Services (APS)
- Product-specific SDKs
- C++, .NET, Python, AutoLISP
- REST APIs for cloud integration
