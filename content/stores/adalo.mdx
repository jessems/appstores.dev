## Overview

Adalo Component Marketplace is the official marketplace for Adalo no-code app builder. With 50+ components and templates for mobile and web apps, it extends Adalo's drag-and-drop platform for entrepreneurs, startups, and businesses building apps without code.

## Key Statistics

- **50+ components** (estimated)
- Component Marketplace 2.0 launched March 2022
- 25+ new components at 2.0 launch
- Growing third-party ecosystem

## Marketplace 2.0 (2022)

Launched with over 25 new components to make Adalo apps:
- Slicker design
- Faster performance
- Better functionality

## Component Types

### UI Components
- Navigation elements
- Forms and inputs
- Media displays
- Charts and graphs

### Functionality
- QR Code Generator (8,000+ installations)
- Payment integrations
- Social features
- Data connections

## Feature Templates

Pre-built feature templates include:
- Component selection
- Styling applied
- Database setup
- Data pre-loaded
- Screens linked
- Ready to customize

Templates are designed to work together, allowing quick assembly of complete apps.

## Third-Party Marketplaces

Beyond the official marketplace:
- **Adalomatic** - Third-party components
- **UIXX** - Themes and data templates
- **itsmi.store** - Additional components

## Development
