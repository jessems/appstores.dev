## Overview

SketchUp Extension Warehouse is the official marketplace for SketchUp's 3D modeling software. With thousands of extensions available, most of which are free, it provides tools for architecture, interior design, construction, and 3D visualization.

- **1,000+ extensions** (estimated)
- Hundreds of free and paid options
