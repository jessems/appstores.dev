## Overview

Freshworks Marketplace serves the entire Freshworks product suite including Freshdesk (customer support), Freshservice (IT service management), Freshsales (CRM), and more. With 1,400+ public integrations and 150,000+ active installations, it's a major enterprise marketplace.

## Key Statistics

- **1,400+ public integrations**
- **150,000+ active installations**
- **500+ global partners**
- **Up to 40% partner margins**

## Supported Products

- **Freshdesk**: Customer support software
- **Freshservice**: IT service management
- **Freshsales**: Sales CRM
- **Freshmarketer**: Marketing automation
- **Freshchat**: Messaging platform

## Partner Program (2024)

Freshworks offers 3 partner tiers:
- Deal registration benefits
- AI training resources
- Up to 40% margins for resellers
- Access to 1,400+ marketplace integrations
