## Overview

Okta Integration Network (OIN) is the largest network of pre-built identity integrations. With 7,000+ integrations, it enables enterprises to connect identity management across their entire tech stack.

## Key Statistics

- **7,000+ integrations** pre-built
- **150+ Secure Identity Integrations** (new 2025)
- **100+ apps** per customer (global average)
- **9% YoY growth** in app adoption
- **40% security tools** among fastest-growing

## 2025 Businesses at Work Insights

- Global average apps per customer topped 100 for first time
- Security tools account for 40% of fastest-growing apps
- Bitwarden and Tailscale lead security adoption
- Drata and Vanta lead compliance tools

## Integration Types

### SSO (Single Sign-On)
- SAML 2.0
- OpenID Connect (OIDC)
- WS-Federation

### Lifecycle Management
- SCIM provisioning
- User sync
- Deprovisioning

### Secure Identity Integrations
New 2025 collection with deep security capabilities for critical apps.

## Development

Build integrations using:
- SAML 2.0 or OIDC for SSO
- SCIM 2.0 for provisioning
- Okta SDKs
- OAuth 2.0 for API access
- Okta Workflows
