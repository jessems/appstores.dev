## Overview

Setapp is a subscription-based platform by MacPaw offering 260+ premium Mac and iOS apps for a single monthly fee. Users get full access to productivity, design, development, and utility tools without individual purchases.

## Key Statistics

- **260+ apps** for Mac and iOS
- Popular apps like CleanMyMac X, Spark, Ulysses
- No in-app purchases or ads
- Automatic updates included

## Subscription Plans

### Mac + iOS Plan
One Mac plus multiple iOS devices.

### Power User Plan
Multiple Macs (desktop + laptop).

### Teams/Business
Per-user subscriptions with centralized billing.

### Setapp AI
Adds AI credits for AI-capable apps.

## Pricing (2025)

- 7-day free trial
- Standard plans from ~$10/month
- 1-year AI plan: $68.99

## Popular Apps

- CleanMyMac X

Apps earn revenue based on:
