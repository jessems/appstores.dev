## Overview

Character.AI is a consumer-focused AI platform that specializes in personality-driven, conversational AI characters. Founded in 2021 by former Google AI researchers, it has grown to millions of users who chat with and create AI characters for entertainment, roleplay, education, and creative exploration.

## Key Features

### Character Creation
Creators can design characters with:
- Unique personalities and backstories
- Custom voices (Voice feature)
- Detailed character definitions
- Greeting messages and conversation starters
- Avatar images

### Character Types
Popular character categories include:
- Fictional characters (anime, games, movies)
- Historical figures
- Original creations
- AI companions
- Educational tutors
- Roleplay scenarios

### Chat Experience
- Natural, personality-consistent conversations
- Memory of previous interactions
- Multi-character group chats
- Voice chat support

## Subscription: c.ai+

The premium subscription ($9.99/month) includes:
- Priority access during high traffic
- Faster response times
- Early access to new features
- Exclusive community features

## Character Discovery

- Browse by category (Anime, Games, Movies, Education, etc.)
- Trending and popular characters
- Creator profiles
- Character ratings
- Search functionality

## Use Cases

### Entertainment
- Roleplay with fictional characters
- Creative storytelling
- Game companions

### Education
- Language learning partners
- Subject-specific tutors
- Historical figure conversations

### Productivity
- Brainstorming partners
- Interview practice
- Writing assistants

## Platform History

- **2021**: Founded by Noam Shazeer and Daniel De Freitas
- **2022**: Public beta launch
- **2023**: Mobile apps released, c.ai+ subscription launched
- **2024**: Voice features added, continued growth

## Getting Started

1. Create a free account
2. Browse or search for characters
3. Start chatting
4. Create your own character:
   - Define personality and background
   - Set greeting message
   - Add avatar
   - Publish and share
