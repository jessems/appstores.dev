## Overview

Azure Marketplace is part of Microsoft's commercial marketplace, alongside AppSource for business applications. It offers ISVs access to Microsoft's enterprise customers with one of the lowest transaction fees in the industry.

## Fee Structure

- **Flat 3% transaction fee** on all sales
- No upfront listing fees
- ISVs keep approximately 97% of revenue
- Microsoft handles tax collection and remittance

## ISV Incentive Programs (FY25)

### Marketplace Transact and Grow
- Revenue tracked by Publisher ID across all listings
- $20,000 rebate at $100K eligible Azure Consumed Revenue (ACR)
- $50,000 additional rebate at $4M eligible ACR

## Integration Benefits

- Customers can use existing Azure commitments
- Simplified procurement through Azure billing
- Enterprise Agreement support
- Co-sell opportunities with Microsoft sellers
