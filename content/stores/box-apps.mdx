## Overview

Box App Center launched in 2022 as the destination for users, admins, and developers to discover and access 1,500+ applications that integrate with Box's content cloud platform.

## Key Statistics

- **1,500+ integrations** across categories
- **Enterprise-grade** security requirements
- Supports productivity, CRM, HR, ERP stacks

## Key Integration Partners

- Microsoft (Office, Teams)
- Zoom
- Salesforce
- Slack
- Google Workspace

## Integration Categories

- Productivity & collaboration
- CRM & sales
- HR & people management
- ERP & finance
- Legal & compliance
- Healthcare & life sciences

## AI Capabilities (2024-2025)

- **Box AI Studio**: Rolling out January 2025
- Deep GPT model partnerships
- AI-governance engine for enterprise

## Development

Build integrations using:
- Box REST API
- Official SDKs (JS, Python, Java, .NET, iOS, Android)
- OAuth 2.0 authentication
- Webhooks for real-time updates
- Box UI Elements
