## Overview

Samsung Galaxy Store (formerly Samsung Apps) is Samsung's proprietary app store, available on all Samsung Galaxy smartphones, tablets, smartwatches, and smart TVs running Tizen.

## Benefits for Developers

### Samsung Device Integration
- One UI optimization support
- Samsung Pay integration
- Galaxy Watch app distribution
- Samsung DeX support
- Bixby integration capabilities

### Promotional Opportunities
Samsung actively promotes apps through:
- Galaxy Store featured sections
- Samsung Members app promotions
- Device-specific recommendations
