## Overview

Zuora Connect Marketplace is the official app marketplace for Zuora's subscription management platform. With 150+ pre-built applications, extensions, and integrations, it helps subscription businesses extend Zuora Central with specialized functionality.

## Key Statistics

- **150+ pre-built applications** (estimated)
- **100+ apps** at marketplace expansion (2017)
- Enterprise subscription platform
- Acquired by Silver Lake/GIC for $1.7B (Feb 2025)

## App Categories

- Quoting & CPQ
- Financial operations
- Data ingestion
- E-signatures
- ERP integrations
- Revenue recognition

## Notable Integrations

- Salesforce CPQ
- NetSuite
- DocuSign
- Avalara
- Togai (acquired by Zuora May 2024)

## Platform Features

- Pre-built applications
- Custom extensions
- Interface tools
- Technology partner ecosystem
- Developer center
