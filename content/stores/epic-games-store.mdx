## Overview

Epic Games Store launched in December 2018 as a competitor to Steam, offering developers a significantly better revenue split. The store has grown through aggressive expansion including exclusive titles and weekly free games.

## Epic Games Publishing

Beyond the store, Epic offers:
- Publishing support for select titles
- Marketing and promotional assistance
- Development funding opportunities
- Epic MegaGrants for innovative projects
