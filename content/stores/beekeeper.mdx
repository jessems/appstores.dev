## Overview

Beekeeper Marketplace is the official integration platform for Beekeeper frontline worker platform. With 52 integrations and 27 first-party apps (80+ total), it extends the digital workplace for frontline workers in hospitality, manufacturing, retail, and construction.

## Key Statistics

- **80+ total apps** (52 integrations + 27 first-party)
- **21% annual growth** in integrations (2022)
- Launched May 2022
- Mobile-first platform

## Marketplace Launch (2022)

The Beekeeper Marketplace was announced as "the next generation of the company's frontline operating system" providing:
- Third-party integrations
- First-party apps
- Customizable digital workplace
