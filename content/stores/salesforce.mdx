## Overview

Salesforce AppExchange, launched in 2006, is the world's leading enterprise cloud marketplace. It offers over 7,000 apps and has seen millions of installs, making it essential for ISVs targeting Salesforce's customer base of 150,000+ companies.

## Partner Programs

### ISVforce Program
- 15% revenue share on AppExchange sales
- Access to Salesforce platform features
- Partner support and resources

### Security Review
All paid apps must pass security review:
- $999 per attempt
- Automated and manual testing
- Typically takes 4-6 weeks
- Free for free apps

## AppExchange Checkout
Salesforce handles billing and payments:
- Bank transfer: 15% revenue share
- Credit card: 15% + $0.30 per transaction
