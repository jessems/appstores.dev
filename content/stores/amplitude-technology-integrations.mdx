## Overview

Amplitude Integrations is the official partner ecosystem for Amplitude's product analytics platform. With 150+ integration partners across 16 software categories, it enables customers to drive digital transformation and product innovation.

## Key Statistics

- **150+ integration partners**
- **16 software categories**
- Launched 2021 with 40+ founding partners
- Grown to 130+ by 2024, 150+ by 2025

## Integration Categories

- Marketing Automation (Braze, Airship, Iterable)
- Attribution (Branch, AppsFlyer, Singular)
- Customer Data Platforms (Segment, mParticle)
- CRM systems
- A/B testing
- Data warehouses
- And more...

## Historical Growth

- 2021: 40+ founding partners
- 2024: 130+ integrations
- 2025: 150+ integration partners
