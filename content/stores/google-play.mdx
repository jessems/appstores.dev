## Overview

Google Play Store is the primary distribution platform for Android applications, pre-installed on all Google-certified Android devices. Launched in 2008 as Android Market and rebranded in 2012, it has grown to become the world's largest app marketplace.

## Key Features for Developers

### Android App Bundles
Google Play supports Android App Bundles (AAB), allowing developers to reduce app size by up to 50% through optimized delivery.

### Play Console
The Google Play Console provides comprehensive tools for app management, including:
- Release management with staged rollouts
- Pre-launch reports with automated testing
- User acquisition reports and statistics
- Revenue and financial reports
- Crash reporting and ANR analysis

### Testing Options
- Internal testing (up to 100 testers)
- Closed testing (unlimited testers via email or Google Groups)
- Open testing (anyone can join)
- Pre-registration for upcoming apps
