## Overview

Gusto App Directory is the official integration marketplace for Gusto's payroll and HR platform. With 180+ integrations and 400,000+ business customers, it connects Gusto with accounting, recruiting, and business tools.

- **180+ integrations** available
- **400,000+ businesses** use Gusto
