## Overview

Volusion Marketplace is the official integration platform for Volusion's e-commerce solution. With 70+ apps and partner technology integrations, it extends Volusion stores with payments, shipping, marketing, and customer engagement tools.

## Key Statistics

- **70+ apps and integrations** (estimated)
- **162 technologies** tracked on Volusion stores
- Built-in comprehensive features
- All-in-one e-commerce platform

## Integration Categories

- Payment processing
- Shipping solutions
- Marketing automation
- Customer engagement
- Accounting
- Analytics

## Platform Philosophy

Volusion focuses on built-in features rather than extensive third-party apps:
- Comprehensive suite included
- Marketing tools built-in
- SEO options included
- Payment processing integrated
