## Overview

Mailchimp Integrations connects Mailchimp's email marketing platform with 300+ apps and services. Acquired by Intuit in 2021, it serves 8 million businesses and maintains 45% market share in SMB marketing automation.

## Key Statistics

- **300+ direct integrations**
- **8M+ businesses** use Mailchimp
- **45% market share** in SMB marketing automation (2025)
- **8,000+ apps** via Zapier
- **1,300+ apps** for contact import via Zapier

## Integration Categories

- Ecommerce (Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce)
- CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive)
- Social media (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter)
- Website builders (WordPress, Wix, Squarespace)
- Analytics (Google Analytics)
- Forms (Typeform, JotForm)

## Mailchimp Marketplace

The Mailchimp Marketplace offers:
- App integrations
- Email templates
- Expert services
- Design resources

## Development

Build integrations using:
- Mailchimp Marketing API
- Transactional API (Mandrill)
- OAuth 2.0 authentication
- Webhooks
- Official SDKs (JS, Python, PHP, Ruby)
