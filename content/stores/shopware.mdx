## Overview

Shopware Store is the official marketplace for Shopware e-commerce platform extensions. With 3,500+ extensions available for Shopware 5 and 6, it provides plugins for payment, SEO, marketing, shipping, and store customization.

- Community Store with thousands of plugins
- Strong European (DACH) market presence
- Both official and third-party extensions
