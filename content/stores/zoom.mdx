## Overview

Zoom App Marketplace launched in October 2018 and has grown from 200 apps to over 2,700 apps and integrations. It supports apps for Zoom Meetings, Webinars, Rooms, Phone, and Team Chat.

## Key Statistics

- **2,700+ apps** and integrations
- **6M+ app installs** total
- **650% growth** in apps since 2020
- **19% AI-powered apps** (growing category)
- **300M+ daily meeting participants**

## App Types

### Zoom Apps
- Run inside Zoom client
- Access to Zoom Apps SDK
- In-meeting experiences

### Integrations
- API-based connections
- Webhook notifications
- OAuth authentication

### Chatbots
- Team Chat integrations
- Automated workflows
- Command-based interactions

## Growth Timeline

- October 2018: Marketplace launched
- 2020: 200 apps available
- July 2021: 1,000+ apps
- 2024: 2,500+ apps
- 2025: 2,700+ apps (19% AI-powered)
