## Overview

HubSpot App Marketplace is one of the most developer-friendly enterprise marketplaces, taking 0% commission from app sales. Launched in 2017, it connects over 1,500 apps with HubSpot's growing customer base.

## App Partner Program

### Key Benefits
- 0% commission on all app sales
- Free marketplace listing
- Developer support resources
- Access to HubSpot's API and SDKs
- Discounted INBOUND event sponsorship

### Certification Requirements
As of August 2025:
- All certified apps follow a 2-year certification cycle
- Apps must meet UX, security, and functionality standards
- Failure to recertify results in loss of certified status

## Monetization

Developers handle their own billing and payments:
- No HubSpot-managed checkout
- Use your own payment processor
- Keep 100% of revenue
