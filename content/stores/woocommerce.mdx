## Overview

WooCommerce Marketplace is the official extension store for WooCommerce, the open-source e-commerce platform that powers over 25% of all online stores. With 8 million+ active installations, it's one of the largest e-commerce ecosystems.

## Key Statistics

- **1,200+ premium extensions** on official store
- **4,600+ plugins** with WooCommerce tag on WordPress.org
- **8M+ active installations** of WooCommerce
- **370M+ total downloads** of WooCommerce plugin
- **30,000 daily downloads** average

## Distribution Channels

### Official WooCommerce.com Store
- Premium extensions
- Vetted and reviewed
- Official support included

### WordPress.org Repository
- Free extensions
- Open to all developers
- No revenue share

### Third-Party Marketplaces
- CodeCanyon: 1,564 WooCommerce plugins
- ThemeForest: 1,500+ WooCommerce themes
- Independent developer sites

## Development Requirements

- PHP and WordPress expertise
- WooCommerce API knowledge
- WordPress coding standards compliance
- Security best practices
