## Overview

PrestaShop Addons Marketplace is the official module and theme marketplace for PrestaShop's open source e-commerce platform. With 3,000+ modules and 30,000+ satisfied customers, it's the central hub for extending PrestaShop stores.

## Key Statistics

- **3,000+ modules** available
- **30,000+ satisfied** customers
- Thousands of add-ons to choose from
- Active global community

## Alternative Marketplaces

Beyond the official marketplace:
- **Codecanyon**: ~300 PrestaShop modules
- **PrestaHero**: 96 premium + 40 free modules
- **FME Modules**: 150+ modules (versions 1.6-1.8)

## Module Categories

- Payment gateways
- Shipping & logistics
- SEO & marketing
- Analytics & reporting
- Customer experience
- Admin tools
- Import/export
- Multi-vendor marketplace

## Supported Versions

- PrestaShop 1.6.x
- PrestaShop 1.7.x
- PrestaShop 8.x (latest)

- JavaScript
