## Overview

AppDirect Add-On Store is the official marketplace for AppDirect commerce platform extensions. With 40+ partner solutions, integrations, and connectors plus 300+ pre-integrated SaaS/IaaS providers, it extends the AppDirect ecosystem for B2B marketplace operators worldwide.

## Key Statistics

- **40+ add-on solutions** in Add-On Store
- **300+ SaaS/IaaS providers** pre-integrated
- **Thousands of SKUs** available
- Launched 2019

## Add-On Store Launch (2019)

Built on AppDirect's industry-leading AppMarket platform, the Add-On Store includes close to 40 complementary partner solutions across:
- CRM
- Finance & accounting
- Marketing automation
- Analytics
- Integration connectors

## AppMarket Platform

The broader AppDirect ecosystem includes:
- 300+ pre-integrated SaaS/IaaS providers
- Immediate marketplace deployment
- Seamless billing and provisioning
- Hundreds of providers, thousands of SKUs
