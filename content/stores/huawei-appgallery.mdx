## Overview

Huawei AppGallery has grown to become the third-largest app store globally after Google Play and Apple App Store. It's essential for reaching Huawei and Honor device users, especially in markets where Google services are limited.

## HMS Core Integration

Huawei Mobile Services (HMS) Core provides:
- Push notifications
- Analytics
- In-app purchases
- Ads monetization
- Location services
- ML capabilities

These serve as alternatives to Google Mobile Services (GMS).
