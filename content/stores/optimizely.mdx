## Overview

Optimizely App Marketplace is the official integration platform for Optimizely's digital experience platform. With 100+ integrations across experimentation, CMS, and marketing automation, it connects Optimizely with your tech stack.

## Key Statistics

- **100+ integrations** (estimated)
- **55+ Make/Zapier connectors**
- Connect Platform for custom integrations
- Growing ecosystem

## Integration Types

### MarTech Connectors
Third-party marketing technology integrations.

### Presentation Layer Apps
JavaScript and tags for CMS and Commerce websites.

### Embedded Apps
Capabilities within the Optimizely editor UI.

## Campaign Marketplace

55+ integrations via Make and Zapier:
- Lead capture
- E-commerce
- CRM
- Customer support
- Social platforms
- Analytics
