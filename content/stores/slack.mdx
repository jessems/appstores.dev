## Overview

Slack App Directory launched in 2015 and has grown to over 2,600 apps serving millions of users. Since Salesforce's acquisition of Slack in 2021, the platform has continued to expand its developer ecosystem.

## Developer Requirements

### Support Requirements
- Clear contact method for support
- No additional signup required for support
- Respond within 2 business days
- Maintain up-to-date documentation

### App Guidelines
Effective December 10, 2024:
- Security best practices
- User privacy compliance
- Minimal permission scopes
- Clear value proposition

## Monetization

Developers handle all billing:
- Choose your own payment processor
- Set your own pricing models
- Keep 100% of revenue
- No Slack-managed checkout
