## Overview

Mac App Store launched in 2011 as Apple's official marketplace for macOS applications. Part of Apple's broader App Store ecosystem that facilitated $406 billion in US developer billings and sales in 2024.

## Apple App Store Key Statistics

- **1.9M+ apps** across all Apple platforms
- **$406B** US developer billings (2024)
- **783,000+ publishers** on App Store
- **30,000+ new apps** added monthly
- **37%** from indie developers
- **110,000 accounts** terminated for fraud

## Developer Economics

- 90%+ of billings have 0% Apple commission
- 15% commission for Small Business Program (<$1M revenue)
- 30% standard commission
- Small developer earnings up 76% (2021-2024)

## App Pricing

- 94.6% of apps are free
- 5.4% are paid apps
- Freemium and subscription models dominate

## Development

Build Mac apps using:
- Swift/SwiftUI
- Objective-C/AppKit
- Mac Catalyst (iOS to Mac)
- Xcode IDE
- TestFlight for beta testing
- App Store Connect analytics
