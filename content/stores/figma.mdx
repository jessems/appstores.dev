## Overview

Figma Community launched in 2019 and has grown to host thousands of plugins, widgets, and design resources. It's the primary distribution channel for extending Figma's design platform.

## Monetization

### Pricing Options
- One-time payments (minimum $2)
- Monthly subscriptions (minimum $2/month)
- Prices must be whole numbers in USD

### Commission Structure
- Figma takes a flat 15% fee
- Covers payment processing and support
- Sales tax collected by Figma (doesn't affect earnings)

### Payouts
- Cash out available 30 business days after sale
- Maximum one cash out per week
- Stripe used for payment processing

## Important Note

Figma is not currently approving new creators for selling paid files. Developers can still monetize through third-party payment pages linked from their plugin UI and Community page.
