## Overview

Adobe Exchange is the official marketplace for Adobe Creative Cloud and Experience Cloud plugins and extensions. It serves millions of creative professionals with add-ons for Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, and enterprise products.

## Marketplace Structure

### Creative Cloud Marketplace
Desktop app Plugin Marketplace and Exchange web surface showcase the same plugins.

### Experience Cloud Apps
Enterprise integrations for Adobe Workfront, Analytics, and other Experience Cloud products.

## Plugin Formats

- **UXP**: Modern JavaScript-based plugin format
- **ZXP/CEP**: Legacy ExtendScript-based plugins
- **MXI**: Older installation packages

## Supported Applications

- Photoshop
- Illustrator
- Premiere Pro
- After Effects
- InDesign
- XD
- Lightroom
- Experience Cloud products

## Monetization Features

- FastSpring e-commerce platform
- Merchant of Record for EU
- VAT collection and remittance
- Free or paid perpetual products
- Subscription support

## Development

Build plugins using:
- Developer Distribution portal
- UXP (Unified Extensibility Platform)
- CEP (Common Extensibility Platform)
- Adobe APIs and SDKs
