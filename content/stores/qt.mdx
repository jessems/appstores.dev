## Overview

Qt Marketplace is the official extension platform for Qt development framework. With 200+ extensions, it provides plugins for Qt Creator, libraries with new APIs, and tools for Qt's ecosystem of over 1 million developers building cross-platform applications.

## Key Statistics

- **200+ extensions** (estimated)
- **100+ extensions** at December 2019 launch
- **1M+ developers** in Qt ecosystem
- Cross-platform support

## Commission Structure

| Year | Commission |
|------|------------|
| First year | 25% |
| Subsequent years | 30% |

## Extension Categories

- Qt Creator plugins
- Libraries & APIs
- KDE frameworks
- Tools & utilities
- Testing solutions
- Build systems

## Notable Extensions

- Felgo (additional Qt APIs)
- Incredibuild (network build system)
- Froglogic (Qt testing)
- KDAB KUESA (workflow software)
- KDE add-ons

## Development
