## Overview

Keap Marketplace (formerly Infusionsoft) is the official integration platform for Keap small business CRM. With 2,500+ integrations and access to 5,000+ apps via Zapier, it connects CRM, email marketing, and sales automation with business tools.

## Key Statistics

- **2,500+ marketplace integrations**
- **200+ native integrations**
- **5,000+ apps** via Zapier
- **100K+ small business users**
