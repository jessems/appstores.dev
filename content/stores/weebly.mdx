## Overview

Weebly App Center is the marketplace for Weebly's website builder platform. With 400+ apps contributed since 2015, it provides tools for e-commerce, marketing, and site enhancement. Note: Closed to new app submissions since February 2020.

## Key Statistics

- **400+ apps** available
- **250,000+ downloads** at launch growth point
- Launched Fall 2015 with 150+ apps
- Acquired by Square

## Important Notice

As of February 28, 2020:
- New app submissions are closed
- Existing approved apps remain available
- Bug fixes and updates still accepted
- Active apps serve existing and new customers

## Historical Growth

- **Fall 2015**: Launched with 150+ apps
