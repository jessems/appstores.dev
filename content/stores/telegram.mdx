## Overview

Telegram Bot Platform powers 10 million+ bots on Telegram's messaging platform. Free for developers, it enables automation, payments, games, and full web applications (Mini Apps) for 900 million+ users.

## Key Statistics

- **10 million+ bots** on platform
- **900M+ users** globally
- Free for users and developers
- Multiple monetization options

## Bot Types

### Standard Bots
Automated programs responding to commands and messages.

### Mini Apps
Full web applications running inside Telegram with rich interfaces.

## Popular Bot Categories

- Trading bots (crypto)
- Productivity tools
- Games & entertainment
- Customer service
- Notifications
- Payment processing
- Group management

## Monetization Options

### Telegram Stars
In-app purchases for digital goods.

### Telegram Ads
Revenue sharing from ads displayed in channels.

### Subscription Plans
Paid access to premium bot features.

### Third-Party Payments
Integration with payment providers.

## Development

Build bots using:
- Telegram Bot API
- BotFather for bot creation
