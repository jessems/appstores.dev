## Overview

Xero App Store is the official marketplace for Xero's cloud accounting platform. With 1,000+ integrations and 4.6 million subscribers worldwide, it connects small businesses with essential business tools.

## Key Statistics

- **1,000+ integrations** globally
- **4.6M subscribers** worldwide
- **506 apps** in US store
- **650 apps** in UK store
- **200,000+ accountants** and bookkeepers
- **8.05% AI-powered** apps (and growing)
