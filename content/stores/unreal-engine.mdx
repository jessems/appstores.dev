## Overview

Unreal Engine Marketplace launched in 2014 as part of Epic's commitment to making game development more accessible. It provides a curated selection of high-quality assets, blueprints, and tools for Unreal Engine developers.

## Asset Categories

### 3D Content
- Characters and animations
- Environments
- Props and vehicles
- Materials and textures

### Blueprints & Code
- Gameplay mechanics
- AI systems
- User interfaces
- Editor tools

### Audio
- Music packs
- Sound effects
- Ambient audio

## Free Monthly Content

Epic releases free assets every month, including:
- Selected marketplace content
- Permanently free assets
- Featured content from creators
