## Overview

JetBrains Marketplace is the official distribution hub for plugins extending JetBrains IDEs. With over 8,860 plugins, it serves millions of developers using IntelliJ IDEA, PyCharm, WebStorm, PhpStorm, and other JetBrains products.

## Key Statistics

- **8,860+ plugins** available
- **2,000+ plugin developers** (from Plugin Developer Conf 2024)
- Supports all JetBrains IDEs
- Perpetual and subscription licensing options

## Supported IDEs

- IntelliJ IDEA (Community & Ultimate)
- PyCharm
- WebStorm
- PhpStorm
- RubyMine
- GoLand
- Rider
- CLion
- DataGrip
- Android Studio (compatible plugins)

## Monetization Options

### Licensing Types
- **Subscription**: Monthly or annual recurring payments
- **Perpetual**: One-time payment for lifetime access (new in 2024)
- **Freemium**: Free tier with paid upgrades

### Revenue Share
- Free plugins: No fees
- Paid plugins: 15% commission to JetBrains

## Plugin Developer Conf

JetBrains hosts an annual Plugin Developer Conference:
- First event: November 2024 with 2,000+ attendees
- Features talks on testing, localization, and user feedback
- Returns November 2025
