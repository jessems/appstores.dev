## Overview

Intuit App Partner Program is the official developer ecosystem for QuickBooks and Intuit Enterprise Suite. With 800+ integrated apps and a new tiered partner program, it powers millions of QuickBooks customer experiences.

## Key Statistics

- **800+ integrated apps**
- **Thousands of integrated applications**
- **20+ years** developer program
- Millions of customer experiences

## New App Partner Program (July 2025)

### Partner Tiers
- **Builder**: No platform fees (US-based)
- **Silver**: Enhanced resources
- **Gold**: Increased support
- **Platinum**: Premium benefits

### New APIs (2025)
- Project management
- Sales tax
- Payroll
- And more

## Program Benefits

- Access to APIs at no charge
- Variable API fees as you grow
- Flat monthly program fee by tier
- Enhanced support and resources

## Development

Build integrations using:
- QuickBooks Online APIs
- OAuth 2.0 authentication
- Intuit Developer Portal
- New 2025 APIs
- Partner program resources
